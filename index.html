More about us

Who are we?

We are a group self taught women software developer.

Our own experience motivated us to open this space and help people around the world to connect.

We want to offer support to other people that like us, are learning at the moment and need guidance from an industry expert.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to offer mentorship for all. We're a platform for people to find, book and meet mentors around the world.

We offer information about events that are happening in different locations.

Our mission is also to offer a platform for professionals that want to offer their knowledge and to help future professionals.

How it works

In our Mentors page you can see the mentors available at the moment.

Once you have picked one, send us a message to request the mentor you are interested in.

We will review your application and we will put you in contact with your new mentor.
