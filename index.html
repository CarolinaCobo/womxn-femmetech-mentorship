Get to know us

How are we?

We are a group self taught software developer women. We want to offer support to other people that as us, are learning at the moment and need guidance.

Our mission

We are on a mission to offer mentorships for all. We're a platform for people to find, book and meet mentors around the world. We also offer information about events that are happening.

How it works

    It's really simple!

  • In our Mentors page you can see the mentors available at the moment.
  • Once you have picked one, send us a message to request the mentor you are interested in.
  • We will review your application and we will put you in contact with your new mentor.
