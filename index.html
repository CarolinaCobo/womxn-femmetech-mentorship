Get to know us

Who are we?

We are a group self taught software developer women. We want to offer support to other people that as us, are learning at the moment and need guidance.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to offer mentorship for all. We're a platform for people to find, book and meet mentors around the world. We also offer information about events that are happening.

How it works

In our Mentors page you can see the mentors available at the moment. Once you have picked one, send us a message to request the mentor you are interested in. We will review your application and we will put you in contact with your new mentor.
